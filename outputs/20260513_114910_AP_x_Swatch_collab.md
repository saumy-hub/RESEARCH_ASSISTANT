# Research Report: AP x Swatch collab

## Executive Summary
Swatch and Audemars Piguet are launching a collaboration called "Royal Pop" on May 16, 2026, with a global in-store synchronized release. The collaboration is expected to bring AP's Royal Oak aesthetic to a more accessible price point, reportedly estimated between $300 and $500.

## Key Findings
- The "Royal Pop" collaboration will feature Bioceramic Royal Oak-styled wristwatches in eight colorways, including white, pink, green, orange, yellow, red, light blue, and navy. (Source: WatchesOff5th)
- The price of the "Royal Pop" collaboration is expected to be around $300 to $500, which is a more accessible price point than AP's premium positioning. (Source: WatchesOff5th)
- The "Royal Pop" collaboration is a first-time luxury crossover between Swatch and a brand outside its own group, with AP being independent. (Source: WatchesOff5th)

## Detailed Analysis
The "Royal Pop" collaboration between Swatch and Audemars Piguet is a significant move in the watch industry. By bringing AP's Royal Oak aesthetic to a more accessible price point, Swatch is aiming to expand its reach and appeal to a wider audience. The collaboration is also seen as a bold move by AP, as it's the first time the brand has worked with a company outside its own group.

The "Royal Pop" collaboration is expected to be launched on May 16, 2026, with a global in-store synchronized release. The watches will be sold in-store only at participating Swatch boutiques, with no online sale at launch. The distribution will be extensive, with 21 US boutiques across 20 cities, 13 UK locations, plus AU, FR, DE, CH, HK, SG, UAE, and Japan.
